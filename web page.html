What is special about coffee?

Coffee is a popular beverage that researchers have studied extensively for its many health benefits,
including its ability to increase energy levels, promote weight management, enhance athletic performance,
and protect against chronic disease.
The healthiest way to take your coffee is hot-brewed and black.
One cup has virtually no calories or carbs, no fat, and is low in sodium.
Black coffee also has micronutrients, including potassium, magnesium, and niacin.
